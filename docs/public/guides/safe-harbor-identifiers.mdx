**BLUF:** The HIPAA Privacy Rule's Safe Harbor method enumerates [18 identifier categories](https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/for-professionals/privacy/special-topics/de-identification/index.html) (45 CFR §164.514(b)(2)). To qualify as de‑identified, all 18 must be removed. This guide walks each category, the formats Masker detects in voice transcripts, and what's full / partial / in‑progress today.

For the live coverage matrix that ships with every audit report, see [HIPAA Safe Harbor coverage](/compliance/hipaa-safe-harbor).

## The 18 categories

For each category we document: shapes detected, default action (`tokenize` / `redact` / `passthrough`), and recall on Masker's internal eval set.

### A. Names

- **Detected shapes:** first names, last names, full names, family relationships ("my mom Sarah"), nicknames embedded in conversational context.
- **Method:** Pass 2 NER (Gemma‑4) — names rarely match clean regex.
- **Default action:** `tokenize` → `MSKV1.person_name.K_HEALTHCARE.<hash>`.
- **Notes:** Tokens are deterministic per session so the LLM keeps using "Sarah" consistently across turns.

### B. Geographic subdivisions smaller than a state

- **Detected shapes:** ZIP (5‑digit + ZIP+4), city names, street addresses, neighborhood names.
- **Method:** Pass 1 regex for ZIPs, Pass 2 NER for cities/streets.
- **Default action:** `tokenize`.

### C. Dates more granular than year (birth, admission, discharge, death)

- **Detected shapes:** `MM/DD/YYYY`, `MM-DD-YYYY`, spoken ("March 14, 1982"), relative ("two weeks ago"), age over 89 (Safe Harbor‑specific).
- **Status:** Partial — year‑only on certain phrasings still passes through.

### D–F. Telephone, fax, email

- Phone: E.164, North American (`(415) 555‑1212`), spoken ("four one five").
- Fax: same regex as phone.
- Email: RFC 5322 simplified.

### G. Social Security numbers

- Dashed (`123‑45‑6789`), undashed (`123456789`), spoken.

### H. Medical record numbers (MRN)

- Tenant‑configurable patterns. Add custom regex in your [mask policy](/configuration/mask-policy).

### I. Health plan beneficiary numbers

- Common payer formats (Medicare, BCBS) — full. Long‑tail payer formats need policy customization.

### J. Account numbers

- Generic numeric + Luhn‑checked credit card detection.

### K. Certificate / license numbers

- Major US states' driver license formats. DEA and NPI partial.

### L. Vehicle identifiers and serial numbers (incl. license plates)

- ⏳ In progress, targeted for May 30, 2026.

### M. Device identifiers and serial numbers

- ⏳ In progress.

### N. Web URLs

- URLs with PHI in query strings are redacted today. Full‑URL redaction in progress.

### O. IP addresses

- v4 and v6, default action `redact` (not `tokenize`).

### P. Biometric identifiers (fingerprint, voiceprint)

- ⏳ In progress. Voice fingerprinting is not in scope today.

### Q. Full-face photographs

- N/A — Masker doesn't process images.

### R. Any other unique identifying number, characteristic, or code

- Best‑effort via NER. By design Masker can't claim "full" on this category.
