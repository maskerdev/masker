Masker is in **private beta**. The hosted portal at [masker-voice.fly.dev](https://masker-voice.fly.dev) and the live demo at [try.masker.dev](https://try.masker.dev) are open today for evaluation. Production beta — with a signed BAA, SLAs, and the ability to deploy in your own VPC — launches **May 30, 2026**.

## What you get in production beta

- **Signed BAA** between your entity and Masker Inc.
- **VPC deployment** option — Rust container in your AWS / GCP / Azure / on-prem environment. Raw audio and PHI never leave your network.
- **Hosted option** — same product on `masker-voice.fly.dev` if you don't need self-hosted yet.
- **All 18 HIPAA Safe Harbor categories** covered (today the hosted portal covers 9 fully + 3 partial).
- **Direct Slack** with the founding team.
- **Pricing:** $0.01 per audio minute, with a $2,500 / 30-day pilot option for early teams.

## Who we prioritize

We can't onboard everyone in May. We prioritize:

1. **Healthcare voice AI builders** with paying customers or named pilots — companies actually placing PHI-bearing calls today.
2. **Series A–C voice AI companies** on Vapi, Bolna, Retell, LiveKit, or Twilio.
3. **Health systems and telehealth startups** running voice agent pilots and needing a HIPAA story before expanding.

If you're pre-product or pre-customer, the hosted portal is open today — use it freely and join the production beta when you have traffic.

## How to apply

Email **[hello@masker.dev](mailto:hello@masker.dev)** with:

- Your company name and what you're building.
- Your voice platform (Vapi / Bolna / Retell / custom).
- Your upstream LLM (OpenAI / Anthropic / self-hosted / other).
- Approximate monthly minutes today, and projected for next quarter.
- Whether you need self-hosted (VPC) or hosted is fine.

You'll hear back within 48 hours. If we can't onboard you in May, we'll tell you when we can.

## What you can do today without applying

- Sign in to [masker-voice.fly.dev/portal/login](https://masker-voice.fly.dev/portal/login) and connect a Vapi agent.
- Run the [Quickstart](/get-started/quickstart) end to end.
- Use [try.masker.dev](https://try.masker.dev) — no signup, no Vapi account required, runs the full pipeline against a stub LLM.
- Read the [HIPAA Safe Harbor coverage page](/compliance/hipaa-safe-harbor) and the [audit report spec](/compliance/audit-reports).

The hosted portal is suitable for evaluation, internal testing, and non-production traffic. It's not yet covered by a signed BAA — that comes with the May 30 production beta.
